    Client Projects

    Geoscience Exhibit

    Collaborators: Dr. Megan Cattau, Mae Bridgeman, Kaj Forney, Chase Franklin, Mitchell Oliarny, Jayden Quallio

    Roles: Programmer, UI, Data Conversion

    About: This application has been developed for an exhibit at the 2024 Personal Structures Exhibition in Venice, Italy. It displays geological drone data gathered by Boise State University's Human-Environment Systems department in an interactive manner. Exhibitors are able to immerse themselves in the environment while learning about invasive plant species in Idaho.

    Games


    Six Shooter

    Roles: Designer, Programmer, Artist

    About: An old-school, VR roguelike, heavily inspired by Spaghetti Westerns of the mid-to-late 1960s. With your trusty revolver in hand, you must face off against waves of outlaws, to determine if you are truly the fastest gun in the west.

    With a focus on fast-paced, arcade-style gameplay, and a variety of levels and upgrades, each playthrough is unique and challenging to the last.

    Repository

    A Thief's Dilemma

    Collaborators: Sam Lummus, Kevin Sherwood, Hank Davidson

    Roles: Project Leader, Lead Programmer, Artist, Animator, 3D Modeler, Level Designer, Sound Designer

    About: A story-based interactive comic book about a master thief who has access to powers related to quantum mechanics. As the thief loses his perception of reality over time, the art style, and the unique games connected to each of the three sections, devolve into further abstract visuals and mechanics.

    Repository


    Temporal Tides

    Collaborators: Konner Pierce, Heaven Rust, Chase Franklin, Jacob Thrall

    Music and Sounds: Ethan Bessette, Bria Eutsey, Quinne Houck, Freddie Oliveira-Chevres, Angelica Ramos

    Roles: VFX Artist, Programmer, Gameplay Implementation

    About: Navigate the treacherous waters of a paradoxical universe aboard a ship adjacent to a massive black hole. Made for a game jam, Temporal Tides explores the theme of paradoxes by having the player's actions be simultaneously reflected and opposed by the echoes from an alternate timeline.

    Websites

    Framed

    Roles: Designer, Programmer

    About: A movie-logging website that allows users to keep track of the films they have watched, as well as the films they want to watch. The website features dynamically updating pages through the use of the TMDb API, as well as a variety of other features such as user authentication, search functionality, and more. Currently in progress.

    Cinema's Wild Frontier: Analyzing the West through Data

    Roles: Designer, Programmer

    About: A narrative website that analyzes the history of Western films, exploring its evolution, themes, popularity, and cultural impact over the decades. The website features a variety of interactive data visualizations, as well as fitting styling and design.

    Dead West

    Collaborators: Chase Franklin

    Roles: 3D Modeler, Programmer

    About: All aboard the interdimensional train. In this chilling hybrid animation experience, you journey through the cabins of an old-western train, witnessing ghostly passengers carrying out a variety of eerie and dangerous tasks. With built in face tracking capabilities, you can actively move the camera to look at specific areas and events along the way.
