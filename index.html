    About


    Hello! I'm Carson, an aspiring game designer and programmer passionate about creating cinematic, immersive gaming experiences that blend innovative storytelling with cutting-edge technology.

    Proficient in various programming languages and game engines, I specialize in systems programming and level design. My diverse portfolio includes VR experiences, an interactive comic, and more emphasizing my creative and versatile approach to game development.

    I view video games as powerful storytelling mediums that can evoke emotions, challenge perspectives, and unite players. My aim is to create games that offer both cinematic narratives and engaging gameplay.

    I'm currently honing my skills at Boise State University, specializing in Game Design, Immersive Development, and Mobile Technology. I'm also engaged in innovative projects like developing applications for autistic children and participating in the NASA SUITS program focusing on biometric and spatial data visualization..

    Outside of game development, my interests in outdoor adventures and film further inspire and shape my approach to game design.
